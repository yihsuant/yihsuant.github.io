1.1 address/ area/ location

1.1.2 方向

east west south north
northwest southeast 直接这么写就可以，不需要加连字符或者大写。

1.1.3 城郊

city center/ town center/ downtown

suburb/ outskirts

urban/ suburban/ rural

urban area (发音连读) = city

*80 *90

四位以内数
